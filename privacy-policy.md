# Privacy Policy for Petrol Peruser

*Last updated: 1 April 2026*

Petrol Peruser is designed to respect your privacy.

## Location

The app uses your device's location to show nearby petrol stations. Your precise location is never transmitted to any server.

## Usage data

Apple may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics through App Analytics. This data cannot be used to identify you personally. You can opt out in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

## Third parties

The app does not contain advertisements, trackers, or analytics SDKs. No personal data is sold or shared with third parties.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, please open an issue at github.com/MaxwellsDemon3142/petrol-peruser/issues.
